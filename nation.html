indian army

Indian Army is the fourth largest army in the world, which is divided into 7 commands. The headquarter of the Indian Army is located in the Indian capital New Delhi and Manoj Mukund Naravane is the Chief of Indian Army.

indian navy

The Indian Navy is the naval branch of the Indian Armed Forces — the other two forces included in safeguarding the country are the Indian Army and the Indian Air Force.“Forged by the sea.” Done!

indian air force
